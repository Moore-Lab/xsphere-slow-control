Each program item is either a step (one or more writes / trackers + a hold) or a sweep (compact "scan one target from start→stop in step increments, dwelling at each"). Step actions and sweep writes execute on entry; the item then holds for its duration before advancing. Sequencer-owned trackers (id prefix seq:) are auto-removed when a later step doesn't re-assert them. At end of program the last step's trackers are left in place; manage them on the Control page.

Sweep targets (scan start → stop)

Compact replacement for the old "Automated gradient scan". For each v in start, start+step, …, stop it writes target = v to every selected target together, holding dwell minutes at each point. Pick one or more targets to sweep them in lockstep. Persists as one program item.
