Starting stops xsphere-slowcontrol so this page can own the LabJack T7 session (the T7 only allows one client at a time). Stopping closes the LJ session and restarts the slow-control service, returning normal operation. Closing this browser tab does not stop the diagnostic — click Stop first. Voltages are polled every cycle; temperatures (LabJack AIN_EF) are polled at ~10 Hz max regardless of the rate setting because the EF read includes the device's internal settling. Sustained ceiling in command-response mode is ~40 Hz for 7 channels (Nyquist ≈ 20 Hz). Spotting 60 Hz mains hum requires stream mode, not yet implemented. The "achieved" pill shows the actual rate.
